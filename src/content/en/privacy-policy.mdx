# Privacy Policy for Devegion

**Effective Date:** October 19, 2025

Devegion ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal
data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information
when you visit our website [www.devegion.com] (the "Site"), interact with our services, or contact
us regarding web development, mobile apps, UI/UX design, or software consulting. We operate in
compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the personal data you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

- Name, email address, subject, and message when submitting inquiries about our services.

Additionally, we use your IP address to determine your approximate location for displaying the
correct language version of our Site. No other personal data, such as phone numbers or company
details, is collected.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for:

- Responding to your contact form inquiries and providing information about our web, mobile, UI/UX,
  and consulting services.
- Displaying the appropriate language on the Site based on IP geolocation to improve user
  experience.

Processing is based on legitimate interests (e.g., responding to communications and site
functionality) or consent where required by GDPR.

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is processed internally and
not disclosed to external providers, except as required by law (e.g., to legal authorities). No
international data transfers occur.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses only essential cookies for basic functionality, such as form submission and session
management. We do not use analytics or marketing cookies. IP addresses are processed temporarily for
geolocation only and not stored long-term. You can manage browser cookies via your settings.

## 5. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your data.
- Object to or restrict processing (e.g., for legitimate interests).
- Data portability (where applicable).
- Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at support@devegion.com. We will respond within one month. For
complaints, contact the Romanian National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing
(ANSPDCP).

## 6. Data Security and Retention

We implement appropriate security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect
your data from unauthorized access. Contact form data is retained for 6 months to allow response to
inquiries, after which it is securely deleted. IP data for geolocation is not retained.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; changes will be posted on the Site with the new effective date.

By using our Site, you consent to this policy.
